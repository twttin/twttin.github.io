Zi Wang

An Interdisciplinary Designer

I see the world as an ecosystem where everything is inherently intertwined. As a designer, I help to elucidate the underlying relationships between people, products and technologies in multiple dimensions in order to elevate the experiences. I dive deep into understanding the needs and motivations of people and connect those with both digital and physical interactions. 

I worked on a wide range of projects ranging from Digital Interface, User Research, and Interactive Experience. I approach problems with my eyes to observe, my mind to analyze and my heart to empathize.

I'm passionate for emerging technologies including Wearable Device, Big Data, Machine Learning, IoT, and Ambient Intelligence. I care about Healthcare, Education, Sustainability and people's Wellbeing.
