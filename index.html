A Multi-Disciplined Experience Designer

I see the world as an ecosystem where everything is inherently intertwined. As a designer, I help to elucidate the underlying relationships between people, products, and technologies in multiple dimensions to elevate the experiences. I worked on a wide range of projects ranging from Digital Interface, User Research, and Interactive Experience. I approach problems with my eyes to observe, my mind to analyze and my heart to empathize.
