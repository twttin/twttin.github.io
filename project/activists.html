synthesis research data

The data gathered from all our research provided solid foundation for insight generation. We used affinity mapping for each stakeholder groups and sorted the quotes into themes.

After multiple rounds of iteration, we arrived at our four main insights and one opportunity statement.

Key insights

Academic Equity

Activists and media both choose to highlight information that supports their cause and underplay other information.

Remote Learning

The empowering moment happens when one realizes one's ability to voice out and influence others.

Learning Over Breaks

People unconsciously operate within pre-determined societal structures and ideologies.

Modified Calendar

Activists need to realize their personal truth, come to terms with their power and privilege in order to fight external perceptions and come together as a collective.

opportunity

How might we extend the New School’s approach of personalized, inquiry-based learning into school breaks in order to improve academic equity?

prioritization

A migration towards balanced calendar contains hard work on many aspects. We as a team of designers, decided to focus on ideation the breaktime utilization as part of our proposal.

ideation

Modular and personalized intersessions during break times from school.

A comprehensive tool for teachers to create and manage intersession learning plans.

A custom take-home package for each student to enable their intersession learning plan.

storyboard

We used a storyboard to communicate the idea visually to our partner at New School SF.

Reflection

The project faced critical challenges from COVID not just on our modes of working, but also on our project focus. While the initial goal was to redesign the calendar, we targeted specifically on the take-home package and the packages ended up critical for the remote learning situation amid COVID. The process was a huge learning experience for me in terms of conducting remote research and also rapidly shifting gears against challenges. These insights are undoubtedly beneficial to a designer in a rapidly changing world today.
