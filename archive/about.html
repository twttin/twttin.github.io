Zi Wang

A product designer with interdisciplinary backgrounds currently based in Los Angeles.

I see the world as an ecosystem where everything is inherently intertwined. As a designer, I help people to elucidate the underline relationships between people, products and technologies in multiple dimensions in order to craft better experiences. I dive deep into the needs and motivations of people and connect those with both physical and digital worlds. 

Previously, I worked at BAM Architecture Studio designing physical environments for people and touched on all phases of design from schematic to final construction. I was also part of the core team to design a station-wide integrated LED lighting system at Los Angeles Union Station. Other projects I worked on include laboratories, offices, schools, hospitals, etc.

I studied Master of Interaction Design at California College of the Arts and Bachelor of Architecture at University of Southern California.

When I was a student, I traveled a lot and took photographs. Nowadays, I enjoy making some woodwork and spend my leisure time with my cat. I'm also a huge sports fan and my favorite team is Liverpool FC. I don't play games that much, but one I really like is Blendoku. If you love to play with colors as I do, give it a try!
